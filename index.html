Who We Are

Building Sustainable Pathways Out of Poverty

Benjamin’s Foundation Kenia emerged directly from a foundational commitment to address localized resource discrepancies facing vulnerable children and families. Based in Kenya, we approach community development through comprehensive structural transformations rather than short-term relief efforts.

By integrating global volunteer expertise alongside local initiative management, we maximize capital transparency and directly execute programs engineered for long-term self-sufficiency.

Our Mission

To create definitive pathways out of structural poverty through holistic education, women's empowerment initiatives, and targeted local community projects.

Our Vision

A self-sustaining future where every child and woman has unobstructed access to educational opportunity, personal agency, and livelihood dignity.

Our Core Organizational Values

Integrity Community Autonomy Radical Transparency Empowerment Ecological Sustainability

Cliford Benjamin

Founder & Project Director

"Driven by a deep vision to create pathways out of poverty, I established Benjamin’s Foundation Kenia to deliver structural resources, functional education, and socioeconomic dignity directly into our community's hands. Our field transparency and hyper-local accountability ensure every international supporter or local advocate makes an immediate, verifiable footprint on the ground."
